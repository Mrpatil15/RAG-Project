# Region Brief: Thane

## Overview
Thane is a major city bordering Mumbai's northeastern edge. It has transformed from an industrial hub into a self-sustained mega-city with luxury skyscrapers, large IT parks, lakes, and premium shopping malls. It is highly popular for premium township developments.

## Key Sub-Localities
- **Thane West (Panchpakhadi, Teen Hath Naka):** Mature, premium residential zones closer to the main railway station.
- **Ghodbunder Road:** A 20km long growth highway lined with massive residential complexes, malls, and corporate parks.
- **Kolshet Road:** A rapidly redeveloping premium residential belt.

## Connectivity
- **Road:** Excellent road networks including the **Eastern Express Highway (EEH)**, **Ghodbunder Road** (linking EEH to Western Express Highway), and **Thane-Belapur Road**.
- **Rail:** Thane Railway Station is one of the largest and busiest stations in the country.
- **Metro:** Upcoming **Metro Line 4** connects Thane directly to Wadala (Central Mumbai). The internal **Thane Ring Metro** is also planned.

## Key Real Estate Projects
1. **Hiranandani Estate (Ghodbunder Road):** A massive self-sustained neo-classical township containing corporate offices, schools, and premium housing.
2. **Rustomjee Urbania (Thane West):** A large, award-winning township focused on community living and education.
3. **Lodha Amara (Kolshet Road):** Spread over 40 acres, features premium high-rise towers and large forest-themed green covers.

## Average Pricing (as of 2024-2026)
- **Average Price per Sq. Ft.:** ₹13,000 to ₹23,000+
- **1 BHK:** ₹60 Lakhs - ₹85 Lakhs
- **2 BHK:** ₹1.0 Crore - ₹1.6 Crores
- **3 BHK:** ₹1.7 Crores - ₹2.6 Crores

## Upcoming Infrastructure & Growth Drivers
- **Metro Line 4 Integration:** Providing direct, high-speed transit to central Mumbai.
- **Borivali-Thane Twin Tunnel:** A planned project that will cut through Sanjay Gandhi National Park, reducing travel time between Thane and Borivali to under 15 minutes.
- **Retail & Lifestyle:** Home to Viviana Mall (one of the largest malls in India), Korum Mall, and major schools/hospitals.

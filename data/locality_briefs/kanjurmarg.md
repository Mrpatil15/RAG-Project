# Locality Brief: Kanjurmarg

## Overview
Kanjurmarg is a rapidly growing suburb in east-central Mumbai. It has transitioned from an industrial zone into a premium residential and commercial hub, often favored by corporate professionals due to its proximity to Powai, Vikhroli, and Thane.

## Connectivity
- **Road:** Excellent access to **LBS Marg** (Lal Bahadur Shastri Marg) and the **Eastern Express Highway (EEH)**. The **Jogeshwari-Vikhroli Link Road (JVLR)** connects Kanjurmarg to the Western Suburbs within minutes.
- **Rail:** Kanjurmarg Railway Station is a key stop on the Central Line of the Mumbai Suburban Railway.
- **Metro:** The upcoming **Metro Line 4** (Wadala-Ghatkopar-Mulund-Thane-Kasarvadavali) will feature a key station at Kanjurmarg, boosting connectivity to both South Mumbai and Thane.

## Key Real Estate Projects
1. **Runwal Forests:** Spread across 15 acres, features multiple high-rise towers, premium lifestyle amenities, and large green spaces.
2. **Sheth Avalon & Sheth Montana (nearby):** Premium high-rises with modern architecture.
3. **Arkade Earth:** Known for eco-friendly design and landscape-heavy planning.

## Average Pricing (as of 2024-2026)
- **1 BHK:** ₹85 Lakhs - ₹1.1 Crore (400 - 480 sq.ft. carpet area)
- **2 BHK:** ₹1.4 Crores - ₹1.8 Crores (600 - 750 sq.ft. carpet area)
- **3 BHK:** ₹2.1 Crores - ₹2.7 Crores (900 - 1100 sq.ft. carpet area)

## Upcoming Infrastructure & Growth Drivers
- **Metro Line 4 Integration:** Expected to slash commute times to central business districts (CBDs) by 40%.
- **Corporate Hubs:** Direct access to Kanjurmarg East corporate campuses (e.g., Lodha i-Think Techno Campus) and Powai's startup ecosystem.
- **Retail & Social:** Close to Galleria (Powai), R-City Mall (Ghatkopar), and major hospitals like Fortis (Mulund) and Hiranandani Hospital (Powai).

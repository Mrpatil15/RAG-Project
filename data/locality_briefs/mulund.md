# Locality Brief: Mulund

## Overview
Mulund, known as the "Prince of Suburbs," is a well-planned, premium residential locality in northeastern Mumbai. It is highly regarded for its wide, grid-like streets, green cover, safety, and rich social infrastructure, making it highly desirable for families and high-net-worth individuals.

## Connectivity
- **Road:** Located at the border of Mumbai and Thane. It is connected by both **LBS Marg** and the **Eastern Express Highway (EEH)**. The **Mulund-Airoli Road** offers direct access to Navi Mumbai (Airoli/Ghansoli IT hubs).
- **Rail:** Mulund Railway Station is a major stop on the Central Line, handling substantial passenger volumes.
- **Metro:** Upcoming **Metro Line 4** runs right through LBS Marg in Mulund, with multiple stations including Mulund Fire Station and Devidayal Road.

## Key Real Estate Projects
1. **Piramal Revanta:** Luxury high-rise towers bordering the Sanjay Gandhi National Park, offering premium amenities and panoramic green views.
2. **L&T Elixir Reserve:** High-end township with modern amenities.
3. **Runwal Pinnacle:** One of the tallest residential towers in the eastern suburbs, located near R-Mall.
4. **Oberoi Enigma & Oberoi Eternia:** Super-premium luxury project developed by Oberoi Realty on LBS Marg.

## Upcoming Infrastructure & Growth Drivers
- **Goregaon-Mulund Link Road (GMLR):** A major upcoming infrastructure project featuring twin tunnels under the Sanjay Gandhi National Park. This will connect Mulund directly to Goregaon (Western Suburbs) in under 20 minutes, bypassing heavy JVLR traffic.
- **Commercial Influx:** Growth of retail spaces, cafes, and co-working hubs along LBS Marg.
- **Hospitals and Education:** Home to top institutions like Fortis Hospital, Wockhardt, and renowned schools (e.g., NES International, Billabong High).

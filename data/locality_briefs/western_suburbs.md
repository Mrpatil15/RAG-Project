# Region Brief: Western Suburbs

## Overview
The Western Suburbs are the cultural, retail, and media heartbeat of Mumbai. This region is home to Bollywood celebrities, massive commercial centers, and beaches. It stretches from high-end Bandra and Juhu to suburban corporate centers in Andheri and Goregaon.

## Key Sub-Localities
- **Bandra West & Juhu:** Ultra-premium beachfront and celebrity enclaves.
- **Andheri (East & West):** A massive commercial and residential hub. Andheri East hosts corporate offices, while Andheri West is known for lifestyle and media.
- **Goregaon & Malad:** Highly active residential markets dominated by large gated complexes.

## Connectivity
- **Road:** Connected by the **Western Express Highway (WEH)**, **SV Road**, and **Link Road**. The **Bandra-Worli Sea Link** links it to South Mumbai.
- **Rail:** Andheri is one of the busiest railway stations in Mumbai, acting as a major interchange.
- **Metro:** **Metro Line 1** (Versova-Andheri-Ghatkopar) connects East and West. **Metro Lines 2A & 7** run north-south along the Link Road and WEH.

## Key Real Estate Projects
1. **Rustomjee Seasons (Bandra East):** Premium gated community near Bandra-Kurla Complex (BKC).
2. **K Raheja Viva (Juhu):** High-end residential boutique apartments.
3. **Oberoi Garden City (Goregaon):** Large township featuring Oberoi Mall, Oberoi International School, and ultra-premium residential towers.

## Average Pricing (as of 2024-2026)
- **Average Price per Sq. Ft.:** ₹22,000 (Goregaon) to ₹75,000+ (Bandra/Juhu)
- **1 BHK:** ₹90 Lakhs - ₹1.4 Crores (suburbs like Goregaon/Malad)
- **2 BHK:** ₹1.8 Crores - ₹3 Crores (Andheri/Goregaon) | ₹3.5 - ₹5.5 Crores (Bandra/Juhu)
- **3 BHK:** ₹3 Crores - ₹5 Crores (Andheri/Goregaon) | ₹6 - ₹10 Crores+ (Bandra/Juhu)

## Upcoming Infrastructure & Growth Drivers
- **Metro Line 3 (Underground):** Connects Andheri (SEEPZ/MIDC) and Bandra (BKC) to South Mumbai.
- **Coastal Road Versova-Dahisar Link:** Will reduce travel times along the western shoreline significantly.
- **Corporate Influx:** Proximity to Bandra-Kurla Complex (BKC), Nesco (Goregaon), and MIDC (Andheri).

# Locality Brief: Vikhroli

## Overview
Vikhroli is a strategic suburb in central-eastern Mumbai. It is defined by its massive mangrove forest cover (owned by the Godrej Group) which acts as the green lungs of Mumbai, and its rapid emergence as a high-end commercial and residential destination (primarily Vikhroli West and Godrej The Trees in Vikhroli East).

## Connectivity
- **Road:** Excellent centrality. Intersected by **LBS Marg** and the **Eastern Express Highway (EEH)**. The **JVLR** begins near Vikhroli, connecting it to Powai and the Western Express Highway (WEH).
- **Rail:** Vikhroli Railway Station is on the Central Line.
- **Metro:** **Metro Line 4** (LBS Marg) will serve Vikhroli. Additionally, it is close to the Metro Line 6 (Swami Samarth Nagar-Vikhroli) interchange.

## Key Real Estate Projects
1. **Godrej The Trees:** A highly acclaimed mixed-use development featuring premium residences, retail, the Taj hotel, and commercial offices in a pedestrian-friendly, green campus.
2. **Godrej Platinum:** Luxury residential project offering panoramic views of the mangroves.
3. **Vaibhavlaxmi Central Park:** Mid-segment high-rise projects in Vikhroli West.

## Average Pricing (as of 2024-2026)
- **1 BHK:** ₹90 Lakhs - ₹1.15 Crores (390 - 460 sq.ft. carpet area)
- **2 BHK:** ₹1.5 Crores - ₹2.1 Crores (600 - 750 sq.ft. carpet area)
- **3 BHK:** ₹2.4 Crores - ₹3.3 Crores (900 - 1100 sq.ft. carpet area)

## Upcoming Infrastructure & Growth Drivers
- **Metro Line 6 (JVLR Corridor):** Will run from Swami Samarth Nagar (Lokhandwala) to Vikhroli, connecting the western and eastern suburbs directly via Metro.
- **Godrej Commercial Expansion:** Continual development of corporate parks (e.g., Godrej One, Hiranandani Business Park nearby).
- **Mangrove Conservation:** Proximity to the preserved Godrej Mangroves ensures long-term air quality and scenic views, a rare commodity in Mumbai.
